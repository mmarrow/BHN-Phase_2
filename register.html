Bright House Small Business Month

Begins November 1st

Bright House Small Business Month aims to bring shoppers and small businesses closer together. Join us by registering your small business to be showcased in our special Small Business website. Together we will encourage friends to stay local and support small businesses.

Prizes:
First 500 win $50 American Express® Card
One Grand Prize winner will win
One Free Year of Service

WhereSMALL BUSINESScanWIN BIG.

Being part of Bright House Networks Small Business Month has its incentives. Not only will you be able to reach more local customers but you’ll also be eligible to win a business booming grand prize. The first 500 businesses to register will receive a $50 American Express® Gift Card. However, one lucky business will be selected at random to win the grand prize – One Year of Free Services from Bright House Networks Business Solutions (up to $1,000/month for 12 months).

What does Bright House Small Business Month mean for your business? After you complete your free registration, you will be part of our Small Business Directory. This directory will be featured in commercials to be seen and heard by thousands of customers in your community. You won’t have to do a thing!

When friends visit the Small Business Directory, they will have the option to search via zip code or category to find your business. This directory will be available all year long – so make sure to come back and update your business accordingly. And don’t forget to include a special offer for friends during the registration process. This will help encourage more visits to your store.

Help spread the word and let others know about Bright House Small Business Month. Download an in-store poster to hang in your store and encourage friends to tag their social media posts using #brighthousebizmonth.
